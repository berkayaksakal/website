Our Simple 3-Step Protection Process

Our service is built around three clear steps that find every weakness and tell you how to fix it. This is how we go from confusion to clarity.

Complete Asset Search

We find every domain, subdomain, IP address, and cloud link connected to your business, including forgotten test sites.

We create a full inventory of everything an attacker could see.

Digital Fingerprinting

We identify the specific web servers and software versions you are running and check for expired security certificates (SSL/TLS).

We flag outdated software that is easy for hackers to exploit.

Brand Protection

We look for phishing websites or typosquatting domains set up to steal your customers and employees.

We protect your name and reputation from fraud.

Code Leak Detection

We scan your code repositories (public and private) for accidentally exposed secrets like passwords, keys, and access tokens.

Stops the most common way hackers get initial access.

Cloud Configuration Audit

We check cloud services (like AWS/Azure) for simple, critical mistakes: open databases, exposed storage, or too many employee permissions.

We make sure your cloud is locked down properly.

Container Safety Check

We identify containers used for applications and scan them for old, vulnerable software components.

Ensure the building blocks of your apps are secure.

Credential Leak Monitoring

We continuously monitor dark web and breach databases to see if any of your employee passwords have been leaked and are for sale.

Get an immediate alert if your password is compromised.

Real-World Risk Score

We take all the findings and assign a score based on how easily a real attacker could exploit them, focusing on business impact.

This is the difference between a minor issue and a crisis.

Phased Action Roadmap

You receive a clear plan telling you exactly what to fix in the next 30, 60, and 90 days, with step-by-step guidance.

Stops the guesswork. You know your next 3 steps instantly.
